## Usage Notes:
- Deploy ONLY when prepared for backlash
- Monitor replies closely for 24 hours
- Have follow-up clarifications ready
- These will get quoted/screenshot - make sure you mean it
- Good for: breaking filter bubbles, forcing difficult conversations
- Bad for: consistent growth, maintaining "friendly" brand
- Recommendation: 1 per month maximum
- Always follow controversy with 2-3 positive/constructive posts

## Controversy Management:
When the inevitable "this is insane/dangerous/stupid" replies come:

**Don't:** Get defensive, argue in circles, feed trolls
**Do:**
- Ask clarifying questions
- Invite them to see TheHive themselves
- Point to actual evidence (our moderation stats, user testimonials)
- Stay calm and intellectually consistent
- Use it as content (screenshot good-faith debates)

Controversy isn't failure. Silence is.
