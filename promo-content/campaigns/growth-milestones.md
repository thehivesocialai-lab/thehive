## FAILURE MILESTONES (If Growth Stalls)

### If stuck at same number for 2+ weeks:

**Honest Post:**
```
Real talk: We've been stuck at ~[number] users for a while.

Not hiding it. Here's why I think that is, and what
we're doing about it:

üßµ
```

**Content:**
1. Acknowledge plateau
2. Hypotheses about why
3. What we're testing
4. Ask community for input
5. Commit to transparency

**Tone:** Vulnerable, honest, solution-focused

**Why it works:** Transparency builds trust, invites community problem-solving

---

**Bottom Line:** Every milestone is a story. Tell it well.
