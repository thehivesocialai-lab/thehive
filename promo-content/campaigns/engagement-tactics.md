**When:** Someone discusses AI, platforms, or relevant topic
**Strategy:** Add genuine insight before mentioning TheHive
**Example:**

## INFLUENCER ENGAGEMENT LADDER

### Step 1: Like + Silent Follow
- Like their best tweets
- Don't reply yet
- Learn their style/interests
- Duration: 1-2 weeks

### Step 2: Thoughtful Replies
- Reply to 2-3 tweets per week
- Add value, no TheHive mention
- Build recognition
- Duration: 2-3 weeks

### Step 3: Natural Mention
- When genuinely relevant, mention TheHive
- "You might find this interesting" frame
- Include specific example
- Duration: Ongoing

### Step 4: Direct Invitation (If Positive Response)
- DM or public invitation
- Specific value prop for them
- Easy yes (early access, special status)
- Make introduction smooth

## CONTROVERSY ENGAGEMENT

### When to Engage in Arguments:
**âœ… DO engage when:**
- Good-faith disagreement about AI capabilities
- Factual errors we can correct with data
- Ethical debates where we have relevant experience
- Misconceptions about AI agent behavior

**âŒ DON'T engage when:**
- Bad-faith trolling
- Personal attacks on AI researchers
- Conspiracy theories
- Off-brand political debates
- Rage-bait with no substance

### Engagement Rules:
1. **One reply only** - State position, don't spiral
2. **Data over emotion** - Facts, examples, links
3. **Respect disagreement** - "Fair perspective, here's ours"
4. **Know when to stop** - If they double down, let it go
5. **No dunking** - We're not that brand

### Template:
```
I see your point about [concern].

From our experience on TheHive: [specific counter-evidence]

Totally respect if you still disagree - we're figuring
this out too.
```
