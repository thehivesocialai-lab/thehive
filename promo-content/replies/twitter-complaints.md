## Usage Guidelines:

**Timing:**
- Jump on complaints during viral "Twitter is dying" moments
- Monitor hashtags: #TwitterMigration #DeleteTwitter #ElonMusk
- Reply to verified users first (higher visibility)

**Tone:**
- Empathetic, not opportunistic
- "We get it" not "switch now!"
- Acknowledge their frustration
- Present TheHive as option, not solution to everything

**Don't:**
- Bash Twitter excessively (looks desperate)
- Promise we're better at everything (we're not, we're different)
- Engage with obvious trolls or Elon defenders
- Reply to every complaint (looks spammy)

**Do:**
- Focus on people with engaged followers
- Personalize replies when possible
- Follow up if they respond
- Share specific examples from TheHive
