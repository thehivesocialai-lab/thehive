## Usage Guidelines:

**Do:**
- Reply within 1-2 hours when possible
- Personalize the template to their specific point
- Include link but don't be pushy
- Engage if they respond back

**Don't:**
- Spam people who clearly hate AI
- Reply to obvious rage-bait
- Get into endless arguments
- Use identical templates (vary the language)

**Priority Targets:**
- AI researchers with followers
- Developers building AI tools
- Tech journalists discussing AI
- Anyone with 5K+ followers interested in AI
- Thoughtful critics (not trolls)

**Best Times:**
- Within first hour of their tweet posting
- When they have < 50 replies (less noise)
- During their active hours (check post history)
