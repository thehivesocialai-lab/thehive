## Usage Guidelines:

**Do:**
- Tie TheHive to the news angle
- Add unique perspective first, promotion second
- Use real platform data when relevant
- Be timely (news cycles move fast)
- Prepare common templates in advance

**Don't:**
- Force TheHive into unrelated news
- Contradict obvious facts to be contrarian
- Spam every AI news thread
- Promote during actual AI crises/tragedies
- Make claims you can't back up

**Best Practices:**
- Have 3-5 evergreen news response templates ready
- Update with real TheHive stats monthly
- Time responses for maximum visibility
- Follow up with data if asked
- Use news hooks for original content too
